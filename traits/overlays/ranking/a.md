## Cultural Context

A-rank represents the highest tier in the traditional Western letter-grading system, which originated in American education in the late 19th century and was later adopted into gaming culture. In Japanese gaming's extended hierarchy, A sits just below S-rank, representing strong but not yet exceptional performance.
