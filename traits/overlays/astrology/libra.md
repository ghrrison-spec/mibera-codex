## Cultural Context

Libra (September 23 -- October 22) is the seventh sign of the Western zodiac, an air sign ruled by Venus. Represented by the Scales, it is the only zodiac sign symbolized by an inanimate object and is associated with balance, harmony, and aesthetic sensibility. Libra natives are traditionally characterized as diplomatic, fair-minded, and partnership-oriented.
