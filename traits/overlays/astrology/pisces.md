## Cultural Context

Pisces (February 19 -- March 20) is the twelfth and final sign of the Western zodiac, a water sign ruled by Neptune (traditionally Jupiter). Symbolized by two fish swimming in opposite directions, it represents the dissolution of boundaries between self and other. Pisces natives are traditionally described as empathic, imaginative, and spiritually inclined.
