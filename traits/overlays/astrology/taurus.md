## Cultural Context

Taurus (April 20 -- May 20) is the second sign of the Western zodiac, an earth sign ruled by Venus. Symbolized by the Bull, it is associated with stability, sensuality, and material appreciation. Taurus natives are traditionally characterized as patient, reliable, and drawn to beauty and comfort.
