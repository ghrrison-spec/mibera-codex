# Music Box Vest

## Visual Elements

![Music Box Vest](https://mibera.fsn1.your-objectstorage.com/components/shirts__z50%20(SHORT)

White tank / vest with text in black that reads “MUSIC BOX”.

Dominant colors: White, black.

## Cultural Context

Jani

## Justification

The Music Box and Ron Hardy are as foundational to house music as the Warehouse and Frankie Knuckles. This vest is a direct artifact from one of the most important clubs in electronic music history.
