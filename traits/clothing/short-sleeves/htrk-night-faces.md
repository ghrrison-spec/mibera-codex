## Cultural Context

HTRK (an Australian rock band)

HTRK (pronounced "Hate Rock," formerly Hate Rock Trio) is an Australian experimental post-punk/art rock duo formed in Melbourne in 2003. Known for atmospheric electronic, noise, ambient pop, and dub influences, inspired by David Lynch and post-industrial sounds.
