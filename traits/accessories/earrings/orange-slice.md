# Orange Slice

## Visual Elements

Slice of an orange facing down.

Dominant colors: Orange

## Cultural Context

Fruit-shaped jewelry became a fashion trend in the early 2000s, popularized by Japanese Harajuku street fashion and later adopted by Western Y2K aesthetics. Citrus slice earrings were a staple of the maximalist accessorizing style that defined both Harajuku and the Milady Maker neochibi visual language. Orange slices also evoke the festival tradition of sharing citrus fruits for refreshment and vitamin C.
