# Rayban

## Visual Elements

![Rayban](https://mibera.fsn1.your-objectstorage.com/components/glasses__z140/chicagodetroit_Rayban.webp)

Rectangular-shaped glasses with clear lenses and thick black frames.

## Cultural Context

Ron Hardy

## Justification

Ron Hardy, the legendary Music Box DJ, helped define the raw, ecstatic sound of Chicago house. His eyewear became as iconic as his mixing style. Putting Ray-Bans in the collection is a direct tribute to the man and the era.
