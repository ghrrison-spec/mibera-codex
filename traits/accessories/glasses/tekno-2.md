# Tekno 2

## Visual Elements

![Tekno 2](https://mibera.fsn1.your-objectstorage.com/components/glasses__z140/freetekno_Tekno 2.webp)

Black retro round glasses, with pure black shades and rusty looking frames in a very dark brown, framing half top part of the glasses..

## Cultural Context

Retro round glasses with half-rim frames evoke both the steampunk aesthetic and the industrial-salvage look common at European teknivals, where gear was often repurposed, patched, and weathered from life on the road with traveling sound systems.
