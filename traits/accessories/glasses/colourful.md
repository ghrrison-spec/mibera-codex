# colourful

## Visual Elements

![Colorful](https://mibera.fsn1.your-objectstorage.com/components/glasses__z140/milady_Colorful.webp)

Retro-style visor sunglasses in orange and yellow.

## Cultural Context

Retro visor sunglasses, also known as shield or mono-lens glasses, were popularized in the 1980s by brands like Oakley and became fixtures of both athletic culture and music video aesthetics. The oversized single-lens design has cycled back into fashion through Y2K nostalgia and internet-native style communities.
