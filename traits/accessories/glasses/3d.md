# 3d

## Visual Elements

![3D](https://mibera.fsn1.your-objectstorage.com/components/glasses__z140/chicacodetroit_3D.webp)

Flimsy glasses with white frames. Left frame is blue and right frame is red.

## Cultural Context

Anaglyph 3D glasses became a pop culture staple from the 1950s through the 1980s, using red and cyan lenses to create stereoscopic depth effects in cinema. They experienced a resurgence in the early 2000s and remain an icon of retro-futurist visual culture.
