## Cultural Context

Prescription eyeglasses with half-rim frames became a hallmark of intellectualism and understated style, associated with figures from jazz musicians to university professors. In the Chicago and Detroit scenes, this look bridged the gap between the academic origins of early electronic music theory and the clubs where it was performed.
