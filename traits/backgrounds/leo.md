## Cultural Context

Leo, the fifth zodiac sign (July 23 - August 22), is a fire sign ruled by the Sun. Symbolized by the Lion, it represents confidence, ambition, and leadership. Leos are known for their bold, charismatic, and warm-hearted nature, often thriving in the spotlight. Generally, they can be proud or dramatic but are loyal, generous, and inspire others with their enthusiasm.
