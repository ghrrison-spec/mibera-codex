## Cultural Context

Sagittarius, the ninth zodiac sign (November 22 - December 21), is a fire sign ruled by Jupiter. Symbolized by the Archer, it represents adventure, optimism, and freedom. Sagittarians are known for their enthusiastic, open-minded, and philosophical nature, often seeking truth and new experiences. Generally, they can be restless or blunt but are generous, honest, and love exploring diverse cultures and ideas.
