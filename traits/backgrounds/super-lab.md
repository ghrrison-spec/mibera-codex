# Super Lab

## Visual Elements

![Super Lab](https://mibera.fsn1.your-objectstorage.com/components/background__z10%20%28SCENE%29/super%20lab.webp)

The room has dim lighting, with 2 control panels attached to a metallic piece. One is bigger with a green light indicator (off) and a red light indicator (on). In front is a clear container with very light blue powder inside and a scale; with the scoop placed on the scale indicating the weighing process.

Dominant colors: Silver / grey

## Cultural Context

Industrial-scale production of synthetic substances — the scale-up from home lab to global supply chain.
