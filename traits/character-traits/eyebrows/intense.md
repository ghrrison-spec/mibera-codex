# intense

thick pair of eyebrows, almost straight in shape and slightly arched upwards at the outer ends.

Dominant colors: black

## Cultural Context

Intense eyebrows sit nearly straight with a slight upward arch at the outer ends, conveying focus, determination, or concentrated attention. This brow shape is associated with states of heightened alertness — the look of someone locked into a task, a set, or a moment.
