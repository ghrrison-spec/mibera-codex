# No Tattoos

## Visual Elements

![No Tattoos](https://mibera.fsn1.your-objectstorage.com/components/tattoos__z125/No%20tattoos.webp)

Represents the absence of tattoos on a Mibera character.

## Cultural Context

The absence of tattoos is itself a meaningful choice in a collection where body art serves as a cultural signal. In many traditions, unmarked skin carries its own significance -- in some communities it signals youth, in others purity or social conformity. The deliberate inclusion of a 'no tattoos' option ensures the trait system can represent characters for whom body modification is not part of their cultural expression.

## Justification

Not every Mibera needs ink. Including the blank option means the tattoo slot is always a choice, never an obligation -- and that choice is just as expressive as any design.
