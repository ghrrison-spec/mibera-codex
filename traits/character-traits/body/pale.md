# pale

## Visual Elements

Pale skin, a very light, fair shade with minimal pigmentation, very porcelain-like.

Dominant colors: Cool white, porcelain

## Cultural Context

Pale describes a very light, fair complexion with minimal pigmentation and cool undertones, often described as porcelain-like. In rave and club culture, pallor became its own aesthetic — the skin of someone who spends nights in warehouses and clubs rather than in sunlight. The cool-toned quality gives pale skin a distinctive visual character under artificial lighting.
