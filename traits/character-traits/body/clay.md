# clay

## Visual Elements

![Clay](https://mibera.fsn1.your-objectstorage.com/components/body__z40/clay__z80.webp)

Slightly tanned skin.

Dominant colors: Warm ochre, muted terracotta

## Cultural Context

Clay as a skin tone name references earth pigments that are among the oldest art materials used by human civilizations worldwide. From Aboriginal ochre body painting to Mayan ceremonial coatings, clay pigments represent a universal connection between the human body and the earth. The warm ochre and muted terracotta tones evoke unfired pottery.
