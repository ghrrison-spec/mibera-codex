# umber

## Visual Elements

![Umber](https://mibera.fsn1.your-objectstorage.com/components/body__z40/umber__z80.webp)

A slightly darker tone than tanned skin, with warm-neutral depth.

Dominant colors: Raw umber, warm brown-yellow

## Cultural Context

Umber is a natural earth pigment historically sourced from Umbria, Italy. Raw umber has been used in cave paintings dating back 40,000 years, making it among the oldest pigments in human art. As a skin tone, it describes a warm, slightly dark complexion with brown-yellow depth — the color of the earth itself.
