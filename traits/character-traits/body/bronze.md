# bronze

## Visual Elements

![Bronze](https://mibera.fsn1.your-objectstorage.com/components/body__z40/bronze__z80.webp)

Bronze skin, darker than a tan with warm metallic depth.

Dominant colors: Warm bronze, golden-brown

## Cultural Context

Bronze as a skin descriptor evokes the warm, metallic-toned complexion associated with sun-kissed or naturally deep golden skin. The name references the copper-tin alloy that defined an entire archaeological age (3300-1200 BCE), spanning many of the civilizations represented in Mibera's ancestral lineages.
