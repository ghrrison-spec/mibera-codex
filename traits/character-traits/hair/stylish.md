# stylish

## Visual Elements

black thick dreads wearing a black / dark grey beanie / head cover.

## Cultural Context

The stylish look combines thick black dreads with a dark beanie or head cover, a look deeply rooted in Afro-Caribbean and reggae culture. Dreadlocks carry spiritual significance in Rastafari tradition and have been worn across African cultures for millennia. The beanie-over-dreads combination became a signature look in dancehall, UK grime, and sound system culture.
