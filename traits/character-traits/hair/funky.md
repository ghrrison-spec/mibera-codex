# funky

## Visual Elements

4 min-buns in golden brown / blonde, tied with black hair-ties. one in the center top, one on the side each, and one at the back.

## Cultural Context

The funky style arranges four mini-buns secured with black hair-ties in a geometric pattern — one center top, one on each side, and one at the back. Mini-buns (also called space buns or Bantu knots depending on cultural context) draw from African hairstyling traditions and were adopted into rave and festival fashion for their playful, sculptural quality.
