# rave black

## Visual Elements

a buzz-cut with textures in black.

## Cultural Context

The rave style is a buzz-cut with visible texture, evoking the practical, low-maintenance hair associated with rave and electronic music culture. Shaved or very short hair was common at raves for comfort during hours of dancing, and became an aesthetic choice associated with the scene's egalitarian, anti-vanity ethos.
