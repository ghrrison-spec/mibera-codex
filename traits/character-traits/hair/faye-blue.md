## Cultural Context

The Faye Blue hairstyle references Faye Valentine from the 1998 anime series Cowboy Bebop, whose dark blue-violet hair became one of the most iconic character designs in anime history. Cowboy Bebop's fusion of jazz, blues, and science fiction made it a cultural touchstone that bridges anime fandom, music appreciation, and the retro-futuristic aesthetic that resonates with crypto-native internet culture.
