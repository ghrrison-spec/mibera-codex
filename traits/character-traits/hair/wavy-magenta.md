# Wavy Magenta

## Visual Elements


## Cultural Context

Wavy magenta hair combines the natural movement of wavy texture with a vivid, synthetic magenta dye — a color not found in natural hair. Fantasy hair colors became mainstream through rave, punk, and alternative fashion, where unnatural color signals membership in subcultures that reject conventional beauty standards in favor of bold self-expression.
