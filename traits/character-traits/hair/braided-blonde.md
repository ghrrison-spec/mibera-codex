# braided blonde

## Visual Elements

deep blonde hair braided on both sides with white hair ties and flat bangs.

## Cultural Context

Braiding is one of the oldest hairstyling techniques in human history, with evidence dating back 30,000 years across African, Native American, Celtic, and Asian cultures. Braids served as markers of tribal identity, social status, age, and marital status. The twin-braid style with bangs blends traditional braiding with modern East Asian hair aesthetics.
