# normal

## Visual Elements

![normal](https://mibera.fsn1.your-objectstorage.com/components/mouth__z90/normal__w10.webp)

a mouth portrayed by a short thin stroke.

Dominant colors: black

## Cultural Context

The normal mouth is a short, thin stroke representing the most neutral possible expression — neither smiling nor frowning, simply present. This minimal rendering reflects the collection's shift to "smol mouths" during development, where the team found that smaller, simpler mouths better suited the character's proportions and style.
