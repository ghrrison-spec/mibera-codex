# Line

## Visual Elements

A simple horizontal line serving as a minimal, neutral mouth expression.

Dominant colors: Black

## Cultural Context

A simple horizontal line serving as a minimal, neutral mouth expression. The line mouth is the most reduced possible representation of a closed mouth — a single stroke that conveys neither happiness nor sadness, just existence. In minimalist character design, this extreme simplicity becomes a statement in itself.
