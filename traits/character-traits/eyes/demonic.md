# demonic

## Visual Elements

![Demonic](https://mibera.fsn1.your-objectstorage.com/components/eyes__z69/Demonic__w4.webp)

plain red eyes with no visible iris. Somewhat demonic and soulless.

## Cultural Context

Demonic eyes appear as solid red with no visible iris, evoking possession, malevolence, or supernatural influence. Red eyes are a cross-cultural symbol of evil or otherworldly power, appearing in folklore from European demonology to Japanese oni. In character design, solid-color eyes with no iris immediately signal that something is not entirely human.
