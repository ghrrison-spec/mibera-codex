# meta eyes

## Visual Elements

![Meta eyes](https://mibera.fsn1.your-objectstorage.com/components/eyes__z69/Meta%20eyes__w3.webp)

"eyes" written in sloppy black text instead of actual eyes

## Cultural Context

Meta eyes replace the drawn eye with the word "eyes" written in sloppy black text, breaking the fourth wall of visual representation. This self-referential technique draws from conceptual art traditions — particularly Magritte's "The Treachery of Images" and the text-as-image experiments of artists like Ed Ruscha and Jenny Holzer. In the context of generative PFP art, meta traits comment on the nature of digital identity itself.
