## Cultural Context

Whirlpool eyes feature bright blue irises containing swirling vortex patterns, evoking ocean whirlpools and the pull of deep water. The whirlpool motif suggests being drawn into something vast and powerful — an experience that parallels the immersive pull of a sound system at full power or the depths of a psychedelic journey.
