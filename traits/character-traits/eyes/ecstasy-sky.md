## Cultural Context

Ecstasy eyes feature heavily dilated pupils, a physiological response caused by the release of serotonin and norepinephrine. Pupil dilation is one of the most visible signs of MDMA and other empathogenic substances, and has become an iconic visual shorthand in rave culture art and photography.
