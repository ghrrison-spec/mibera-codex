## Cultural Context

True love eyes combine a pink iris with heart-shaped details, creating an expression of deep, romantic infatuation. Unlike the more cartoonish heart-pupil eyes, true love eyes integrate the heart motif into a more naturalistic iris, suggesting a state of being genuinely, profoundly in love rather than momentarily smitten.
