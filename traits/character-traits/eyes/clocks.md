# clocks

## Visual Elements

![Time](https://mibera.fsn1.your-objectstorage.com/components/eyes__z69/Time__w5.webp)

a pair of bright yellow eyes showing a clock from each one of them.

## Cultural Context

Clock eyes replace the iris with a timepiece motif, evoking the relationship between perception and temporality. Clock iconography in the eyes references the distorted experience of time under altered states — minutes that stretch into hours on the dance floor, or the compressed urgency of a peak experience. The motif also recalls the clock-eyed characters in surrealist and psychedelic art.
