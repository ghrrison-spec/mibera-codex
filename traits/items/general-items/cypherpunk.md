# cypherpunk

## Visual Elements

a white sheet full of short straight vertical strokes in shades of black, with different sizes and lengths.

Dominant colors: white, shades of black.

## Cultural Context

The cypherpunk movement, active since the late 1980s, advocated for widespread use of strong cryptography as a route to social and political change. Its members and ideas directly produced PGP, Bitcoin, and the broader crypto ecosystem that Berachain is part of. The visual of dense vertical strokes suggests encoded or encrypted text.
