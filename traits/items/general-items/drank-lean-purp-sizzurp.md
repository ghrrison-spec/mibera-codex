## Cultural Context

Lean, also known as purple drank or sizzurp, is a mix of codeine cough syrup, soda, and candy popularized in Houston's hip-hop scene in the 1990s. It represents the intersection of drug culture and music that mirrors the rave scene's own relationship with substances.
