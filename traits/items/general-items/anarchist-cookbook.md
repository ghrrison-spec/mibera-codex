# anarchist cookbook

## Visual Elements

a black book with white text that reads “ANARCHIST COOKBOOK”, and significantly smaller red text under it.

Dominant colors: black, white, red

## Cultural Context

The Anarchist Cookbook is a controversial 1971 manual covering everything from drug synthesis to explosives. It became an emblem of anti-establishment DIY culture, though its author later disavowed it, making it a complicated symbol of countercultural knowledge-sharing.
