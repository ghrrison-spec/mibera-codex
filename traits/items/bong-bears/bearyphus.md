# Bearyphus

## Visual Elements

Light grey bear wearing a plain brown bodysuit and darker brown robe tied around their waist and a bong attached to it. On their hand is a white rollie / joint. ## Cultural Context

Bearyphus is a named character from the Bong Bears collection, a bear-pun take on Sisyphus, the figure from Greek mythology condemned by the gods to eternally roll a boulder uphill only to watch it roll back down. The absurdist philosopher Albert Camus famously reimagined Sisyphus as a symbol of human perseverance, concluding "one must imagine Sisyphus happy." Bong Bears is one of the foundational NFT collections in the Berachain ecosystem, and Bearyphus's robed, monk-like appearance with an attached bong leans into the philosophical absurdity of the reference.
