# Bong Bear 86

## Visual Elements

Lighter brown bear wearing a blue headband with marijuana leaves patterns on it in darker shade of blue, and a blue long-sleeves jacket with white stripes on each sleeve. The bear is wearing a white t-shirt with lilac marijuana leaves as its pattern, as well as a golden neck chain with peace-sign symbol under the jacket, and is holding a thin brown rollie / joint. A plushie based on [this Bong Bear](https://opensea.io/assets/ethereum/0x495f947276749ce646f68ac8c248420045cb7b5e/66075445032688988859229341194671037535804503065310441849644897955598871560193).

Dominant colors: Brown, blue, white, lilac.

## Cultural Context

Bong Bears is one of the earliest and most recognized NFT collections in the Berachain ecosystem, created as bear-themed characters on Ethereum's OpenSea platform before Berachain launched its own chain. The collection features a roster of stylized, cannabis-culture-inspired bears each with unique outfits, accessories, and color schemes. Bong Bears helped establish the bear identity that became central to Berachain's community branding, making them a foundational piece of the ecosystem's cultural history.
