## Cultural Context

Bearmo is a named character from the Bong Bears collection, a bear-pun riff on Elmo, the beloved red Muppet from Sesame Street known for his high-pitched voice and childlike curiosity. Bong Bears is one of the foundational NFT collections in the Berachain ecosystem, and its named characters like Bearmo represent unique one-of-a-kind pieces within the broader roster. The playful portmanteau of "bear" and "Elmo" is characteristic of the collection's approach to pop-culture remixing through a cannabis-bear lens.
