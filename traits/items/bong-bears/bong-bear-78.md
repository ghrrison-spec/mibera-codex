# Bong Bear 78

## Visual Elements

Dark brown with ear piercings consisting of two studs and a ring, and a nose ring. It’s wearing a cap and a scarf as a top of the same color and pattern; fluid lines of pastel blue, yellow, red, and green, both with a marijuana leaf in magenta in the center. The brown is holding a brown joint / rollie. A plushie based on [this Bong Bear](https://opensea.io/assets/ethereum/0x495f947276749ce646f68ac8c248420045cb7b5e/66075445032688988859229341194671037535804503065310441849644897946802778537985).

Dominant colors: Dark brown, bright pastel red, purple, blue, yellow, and green.

## Cultural Context

Bong Bears is one of the earliest and most recognized NFT collections in the Berachain ecosystem, created as bear-themed characters on Ethereum's OpenSea platform before Berachain launched its own chain. The collection features a roster of stylized, cannabis-culture-inspired bears each with unique outfits, accessories, and color schemes. Bong Bears helped establish the bear identity that became central to Berachain's community branding, making them a foundational piece of the ecosystem's cultural history.
