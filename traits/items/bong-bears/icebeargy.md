# Icebeargy

## Visual Elements

White bear with hue of blue and a head made of ice-like material, with four spikes-like shape on top and a crescent moon symbol on its chest, holding a white blunt / rollie. A plushie based on [this Bong Bear](https://opensea.io/item/ethereum/0x495f947276749ce646f68ac8c248420045cb7b5e/66075445032688988859229341194671037535804503065310441849644897975390080860161).

Dominant colors: White, very light blue.

## Cultural Context

Icebeargy is a named character from the Bong Bears collection, designed as an elemental ice bear with crystalline spikes and a crescent moon chest symbol. Bong Bears is one of the foundational NFT collections in the Berachain ecosystem, featuring uniquely designed bear characters on Ethereum's OpenSea platform. The ice motif and celestial marking give Icebeargy a mystical, elemental quality that sets it apart from the collection's more streetwear-focused bears, evoking fantasy archetypes of frost elementals and lunar guardians.
