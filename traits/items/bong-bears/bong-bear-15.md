# Bong Bear 15

## Visual Elements

White bear wearing a black cap and a tshirt, ‘3D glasses’ with white frames and red glass on one and blue glass on the other side. The cap and t-shirt have a graphic of a white triangle in the middle, and a stroke of rainbow going towards and through it from one side. A plushie based on [this Bong Bear](https://opensea.io/assets/ethereum/0x495f947276749ce646f68ac8c248420045cb7b5e/66075445032688988859229341194671037535804503065310441849644897877533545988097).

Dominant colors: Black, red, blue.

## Cultural Context

Bong Bears is one of the earliest and most recognized NFT collections in the Berachain ecosystem, created as bear-themed characters on Ethereum's OpenSea platform before Berachain launched its own chain. The collection features a roster of stylized, cannabis-culture-inspired bears each with unique outfits, accessories, and color schemes. Bong Bears helped establish the bear identity that became central to Berachain's community branding, making them a foundational piece of the ecosystem's cultural history.
