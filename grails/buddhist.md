## Cultural Context

The Medicine Buddha (Bhaiṣajyaguru) is the Buddha of healing, central to Mahayana and Vajrayana traditions. Thangka are Tibetan Buddhist scroll paintings used as meditation aids and teaching tools. Amrita is the nectar of immortality in Buddhist and Hindu tradition — Mike Crowley's "Secret Drugs of Buddhism" argues it may have been a psychoactive sacramental drink. Blue lotus (Nymphaea caerulea) is a psychoactive plant with historical ritual use.
