## Cultural Context

Kali is the Hindu goddess of destruction, time, and transformation — often depicted holding a severed head, representing the destruction of ego. Scorpio's three stages of evolution are traditionally represented as the scorpion (base instinct), the eagle (rising above), and the phoenix (rebirth through transformation). Charlotte Fang is the founder of Milady Maker, one of the projects most closely tied to the Mibera universe.

## Visual Elements

Kali depicted as Scorpio. Scorpion item. Eagle feather earrings. Background reminiscent of a phoenix — together the three symbols communicate the Scorpio journey from scorpion to eagle to phoenix. The severed head is Charlotte Fang's, representing the destruction of her ego.

Every Mibera carries three zodiac placements (sun, moon, rising) as modifier signals in the identity hierarchy. The 12 zodiac grails each represent one sign as its own identity.
