## Cultural Context

Themis is the Greek Titan goddess of divine law, justice, and order — often depicted holding a sword and scales. The ouroboros (a serpent eating its own tail) is an ancient symbol of cyclical renewal and balance. Libra is ruled by Venus (221.23 Hz), the planet of harmony and beauty. Libra season falls in autumn (September 23 – October 22).

## Visual Elements

Themis depicted as Libra. Ouroboros on the sword hilt. 221.23 Hz Venus (Libra's ruling planet) frequency on the sword pommel. Plain background composition brings the figure into focus, communicating truth and clarity. Colors evocative of Libra's autumn season.
