## Cultural Context

Dionysus (Roman: Bacchus) is the god of wine, fertility, and ritual madness. The painting referenced in the background is "Mercury Entrusting the Infant Bacchus to the Nymphs" — depicting Hermes delivering the infant Dionysus to be raised in secret. Poppies are associated with Demeter and agricultural fertility. Taurus is traditionally linked to Dionysus through their shared associations with earthly pleasure and indulgence.

## Visual Elements

Femme Dionysus depicted as Taurus. "Mercury Entrusting Bacchus to the Nymphs" painting in the background. Poppies in the foreground.
