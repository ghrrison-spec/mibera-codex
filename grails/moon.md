## Cultural Context

Selene is the Greek Titan goddess of the Moon, typically depicted driving a silver chariot across the night sky. She is distinct from Artemis (the huntress associated with the Moon) — Selene is the Moon personified. 210.42 Hz is the Moon's Cosmic Octave frequency. The speaker cone references sound culture, tying the piece to the music-rooted identity of the Mibera project.
