## Cultural Context

Greek amphorae are two-handled vessels, often decorated with mythological scenes in red-figure or black-figure pottery styles. Bacchus (Dionysus) is the god of wine and ritual madness. Apollo is the god of light, music, and prophecy. Both figure prominently in Greek ceramic art.

## Visual Elements

Greek amphora-inspired aesthetics. Bacchus and Apollo in the background along with figures from assorted pieces.
