## Cultural Context

"Lord Shiva Grinding Bhang" is a miniature painting by Kailash Raj depicting Shiva preparing bhang (a cannabis preparation). Bhang has been used in Hindu religious practice for centuries, particularly during Holi and Maha Shivaratri. Shiva is traditionally depicted with a crescent moon on his head, representing the god Chandra. The river Ganga (Ganges) is said to flow from Shiva's matted hair — he caught the river's descent from heaven to soften its impact on earth.
