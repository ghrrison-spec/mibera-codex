## Cultural Context

Salvador Dalí produced a series of zodiac gouache paintings, including a Gemini piece depicting mirrored twin figures. Gemini is the sign of duality — ruled by Mercury, the planet of communication and exchange.

## Visual Elements

A Mibera and a Milady as the Gemini twins. Mibera and Milady Maker exist as counterparts — two projects that mirror and complement each other — making the pairing a literal expression of Gemini's duality. Composition inspired by Dalí's Gemini gouache painting. 141.27 Hz frequency of Mercury (Gemini's ruling planet) in the background.

## Justification

Every Mibera carries three zodiac placements (sun, moon, rising) as modifier signals in the identity hierarchy. The 12 zodiac grails each represent one sign as its own identity.
