## Cultural Context

Leila Waddell (1880–1932) was an Australian violinist and occultist who became one of Aleister Crowley's most prominent associates, serving as his "Scarlet Woman." The Thoth Tarot was designed by Crowley with artist Lady Frieda Harris — the Lust card (XI) is the Thoth deck's equivalent of the traditional Strength card. The Sigil of Baphomet is the official insignia of the Church of Satan. The Temple of Set was founded in 1975 by Michael Aquino as a breakaway from the Church of Satan. Leviathan, Lilith, and Babalon are figures from various occult traditions.

## Visual Elements
