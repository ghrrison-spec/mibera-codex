## Cultural Context

Chen Hongshou (1598–1652) was a late Ming dynasty painter known for figure paintings with elongated, stylized forms. "Preparing the Elixir Beneath a Pine" depicts Daoist alchemical practice — the pursuit of immortality through elixirs. Opium has deep historical ties to China, culminating in the Opium Wars (1839–1860). Yin yang is the Chinese philosophical concept of complementary duality — opposing forces that are interconnected.

## Visual Elements

Inspired by Chen Hongshou's "Preparing the Elixir Beneath a Pine." Opium poppy in the background. Yin yang reflected in the overlay.
